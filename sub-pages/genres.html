There are several different genres of video games. Some video games focus on fast paced action, while others are made to be calm and relaxing. Here are some examples of video game genres.

- Adventure Adventure games focus on story and exploration. These games usually have the player traverse the world, solving puzzles, fighting enemies, and completing quests to progress the story forward. Adventure games usually have plenty of interesting characters and places to explore. Popular examples of adventure games

    • The Legend of Zelda
      God of War
      The Last of Us
      Uncharted

    • - Platformers Platformer games are a style of game where you traverse through a world, jumping between platforms, avoiding obstacles, and fighting enemies. Platformer games can vary in several aspects. Some have a focus on exploration, while others focus on completing a given task. Popular Examples of Platforming Games

    • Super Mario Bros
      Sonic the Hedgehog
      Hollow Knight
      Metroid

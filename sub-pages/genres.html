There are several different genres of video games. Some video games focus on fast paced action, while others are made to be calm and relaxing. Here are some examples of video game genres.

- Adventure

Adventure games focus on story and exploration. These games usually have the player traverse the world, solving puzzles, fighting enemies, and completing quests to progress the story forward. Adventure games usually have plenty of interesting characters and places to explore. Popular examples of adventure games

  • The Legend of Zelda
  • God of War
  • The Last of Us
  • Uncharted

- Platformer

Platformer games are a style of game where you traverse through a world, jumping between platforms, avoiding obstacles, and fighting enemies. Platformer games can vary in several aspects. Some have a focus on exploration, while others focus on completing a given task. Popular Examples of Platforming Games

  • Super Mario Bros
  • Sonic the Hedgehog
  • Hollow Knight
  • Metroid

- Simulation

Simulation games come in a wide variety of styles. The one thing that connects them is that they all mimic some real-world activity or environment. Some have you raise a family while others have you work on a farm. Some simulators are chaotic, while others are calm and relaxing. Popular Examples of Simulation Games

  • Stardew Valley
  • Goat Simulator
  • Powerwashing Simulator
  • Animal Crossing

- Shooter

Shooters come in two varieties, first person, and third person. While some shooters are single player, most are online multiplayer games. Shooter games usually involve some type of PvP, or player versus player mode. Shooters tend to have short rounds of gameplay making them easy to play quickly with friends. Popular Examples of Shooter Games

  • Fortnite
  • Call of Duty
  • Halo
  • PUBG

- Sports and Racing

Sports and racing games are fast paced and require skill. Sports games are designed to mimic the strategy involved in playing different sports. From soccer to baseball to football, sports games are designed to give a fun and entertaining experience to the player. Racing games allow the player to feel the control and precision needed when driving a fast car. Popular Examples of Sports and Racing Games

  • Fifa
  • Madden
  • Rocket League
  • Mario Kart
